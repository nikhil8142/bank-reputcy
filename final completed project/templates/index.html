What is Bankruptcy?

Bankruptcy is a legal proceeding involving a person or business that is unable to repay their outstanding debts. It provides a fresh financial start for individuals and companies by either eliminating debts or restructuring them under the protection of the court.

What is the Use of Bankruptcy?

Bankruptcy helps individuals and businesses who are overwhelmed by debt. It provides financial relief by stopping collection actions, wage garnishments, and legal judgments. Additionally, it can help in reorganizing debts so that they are more manageable. However, it also impacts credit scores and financial credibility, so it should be considered carefully.
